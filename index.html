What is a WebRing?

A WebRing is a collection of websites linked together in a circular structure. In the early days of the web, WebRings were a popular way to discover related websites. Each site in the ring includes a navigation widget that lets visitors travel to the next or previous site in the ring.

In this way, the WebRing increases the visibility of your website by way of placing it on the first page of Google search results or other search engines. The WebRing also increases the traffic on your website by way of other websites linking to your website. For a more detailed explanation, please refer to the benefits page.

The SKULE™ WebRing revives this tradition for the UofT community, creating a network of personal websites by students and alumni. The current UofT community and Alumni network is thousands-strong—why not harness this immense network?
