What is a Webring?

A webring is a collection of websites linked together in a circular structure. In the early days of the web, webrings were a popular way to discover related websites. Each site in the ring includes a navigation widget that lets visitors travel to the next or previous site in the ring.

The Currently Unnamed Webring revives this tradition for the UofT community, creating a network of personal websites by students and alumni.

The current UofT community and Alumni network is thousands-strong—why not harness this immense network?
