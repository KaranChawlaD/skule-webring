Show Off Your SKULE WebRing Badge!

Proudly display your SKULE WebRing badge on your website or under your email signoff! Below are suggestions and instructions for how to do this, plus some ready-to-use badge images.

How to Add a Badge to Your Website

Copy and paste the following HTML where you want the badge to appear (for example, in your website footer):

<a href="https://webring.skule.ca" target="_blank">
    <img src="https://webring.skule.ca/img/webring-member.png" alt="SKULE Webring Member Badge" width="88" height="31" />
</a>

If you want to use the larger button, you can use:

<a href="https://webring.skule.ca" target="_blank">
    <img src="https://webring.skule.ca/img/join-the-webring.png" alt="Join the SKULE Webring Banner" width="251" height="31" />
</a>

How to Add a Badge to Your Email Signature

Most email clients allow you to add an image and a link to your signature. Here is an example of how your email signature might look:

Kind regards,
Ayan Ali
University of Toronto Flight Endurance Group

To include both badges in your email signature, use the following HTML:

<a href="https://webring.skule.ca" target="_blank">
    <img src="https://webring.skule.ca/img/webring-member.png" alt="SKULE Webring Member Badge" width="88" height="31" style="vertical-align: middle;" />
</a>
<a href="https://webring.skule.ca" target="_blank">
    <img src="https://webring.skule.ca/img/join-the-webring.png" alt="Join the SKULE Webring Banner" width="251" height="31" style="vertical-align: middle; margin-left: 4px;" />
</a>

What Link Should I Use?

When adding a badge to your email signature or website, always link to https://webring.skule.ca so others can discover and join the SKULE WebRing.
